What is "Psych Cat"?

Psych Cat is an experimental platform for creative expression.

We are a collective of individuals who seek to open doors within our community by curating art exhibitions, booking tours, and showcasing local talent!

From zine releases to pop-up shows, we aim to bring awareness to the wide variety of artistic outlets and souls that make up our Psych Cat World.
