Lisandra Valdes
My work mostly consists of linear and organic forms intermingling with themselves. Some of my work is based off of things I see in the real world, then abstracted into my own interpretation using colors and somewhat recognizable forms. I want my audience to be immersed in the moments I create, to forget about everything they’ve ever known and only be within my work.
Mvtchi
Mvtchi uses acrylic paint as a medium focus in her experimental mixed media artwork. She layers a multitude of marks, colors, and to build a complete story in each piece, like chapters in a book, or events in a day. She prefers a raw approach to emotive art, letting the voices of her subconscious become fully exposed, combining many different techniques, and whatever comes naturally. Mvtchi also spends time writing poetry and journals, leaving each painting accompanied with it's own personal verse, for a deeper expression and understanding of the piece. Mvtchi uses art as a tool to heal herself during fits of trauma, anxiety, and pain, and to celebrate in life's precious moments of love, gratitude, hope, and empowerment. Be true to your art, your art will be true to you.
Pueblo de Paz
Pueblo DePaz is not just an alter ego: it is also a place where one can look back and reflect upon. From all his travels, it’s a place where he can call home. Pueblo DePaz, also known as Pablo Emilio Ramos-Nieves is a mixed media artist currently living in Orlando, Florida, USA. His style referred as “Urban Surrealism”: which describes a process of combining strange graffiti elements with culture and fantasy. He is finding new ways of how to look at art, enhancing the brain of how to think and also shifting stomach organs of how to feel about art.
GWADCIP$
Roy Velazquez aka GWADCIP$ is a producer based out of the Central Florida area. He makes LoFi experimental music using digital hardware, all sample based music. He has only been making beats / producing for a year and has truly found his calling. Drawing inspiration from all kinds of music but his most recent inspiration has come from listening to producers like Flying Lotus, Teebs, and Clams Casino. His music tells a story drawing from his own life experiences and he hopes that it evokes an emotional, even nostalgic, response for his listeners.

John Alamo
John Alamo is an abstract artist who has been painting for the past three years. Originally from Haiti, his work is a mixture of magic and voodoo. His process dependso n his environment. With each stroke he expresses heightened energy and emotions. He plays and fights with his own demons producing shamanistic vibes and bringing about a higher consciousness. John wants his work to inspire the audience to become aware of who they are and how they can use their voice to express themselves.
